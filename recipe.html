Lavender Lemonade Recipe

Lavender has many benefits such as reducing anxiety, improves brain function and sleep, restores skin, and protects against diabetic symptoms. This refreshing drink is perfect for summer and along with the awesome benefits tastes great!

Ingredients

  • 6 cups water, divided
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4cup honey
  • 3 Tbsp dried lavender
  • 2 cups lemon juice
  • Lemon slices and lavender peels (for decorations)
  • Blue or purple food coloring for decoration
